SquadShuffle is a powerful team division app designed to help you organize players into balanced teams quickly and fairly. Whether you're organizing sports games, training sessions, or group activities, SquadShuffle makes team creation simple and efficient.

Key Features

  • Create and manage multiple squads
  • Track player skill levels (1-10 scale)
  • Four intelligent team division algorithms
  • Organize gaming sessions with selected players
  • Sort players by name or skill level
  • All data stored locally on your device

üöÄ Getting Started

1. Create a Squad

Start by creating your first squad from the home screen. A squad is a group of players you regularly organize into teams.

2. Add Players

Add players to your squad with their names and skill levels (1-10, where 10 is the highest skill).

3. Create a Session

Create a gaming session, select players from your squad, and divide them into balanced teams using one of four algorithms.

‚öôÔ∏è Team Division Algorithms

Random

Players are randomly assigned to teams. Great for casual games where you want unpredictability.

Balanced

Teams are balanced by total skill level for fair competition. The algorithm distributes players to ensure each team has similar combined skill levels.

Skill Grouped

Players with similar skill levels are grouped together. Beginners play with beginners, experts with experts. Teams are always balanced in size.

Tiered Balanced

Creates balanced teams within skill tiers. For example, if dividing into 4 teams, you get 2 advanced teams and 2 beginner teams, each balanced within their tier.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data synced across devices?

No, all data is stored locally on your device for privacy and offline access. Your squads and sessions are not synced to any cloud service.

Can I edit a player's information?

Yes! Simply tap on any player card to edit their name or skill level.

Can I change a squad or session name?

Yes, tap the edit icon (pencil) next to the squad or session name to rename it.

What happens if I divide players unevenly?

The app automatically creates balanced team sizes. For example, 14 players into 4 teams creates two teams with 4 players and two teams with 3 players.

Can I use this for non-sports activities?

Absolutely! SquadShuffle works for any activity where you need to divide people into balanced groups - study groups, project teams, game nights, etc.

üîí Privacy & Data

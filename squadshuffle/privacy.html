Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 23, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to SquadShuffle. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. This Privacy Policy explains how SquadShuffle handles your information.

Simple Summary: SquadShuffle does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information you enter stays on your device only.

Information We Collect

We collect NO personal information.

What We Don't Collect:

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers
  • No usage analytics or statistics
  • No crash reports
  • No cookies or tracking technologies
  • No advertising identifiers

How Your Data is Stored

All data you enter into SquadShuffle (squad names, player names, skill levels, session information) is stored locally on your device only using iOS's secure local storage.

Local Storage Means:

  • Data never leaves your device
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • No data is stored on our servers (we don't have any servers)
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • Data is automatically deleted if you uninstall the app

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties because we don't collect any data to share.

SquadShuffle does not integrate with:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage services
  • Any third-party services or APIs

Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by:

  • iOS's built-in security features
  • Your device's passcode/biometric authentication
  • iOS data encryption

We recommend keeping your device secure with a strong passcode and enabling device encryption for maximum data protection.

Children's Privacy

SquadShuffle does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is designed to be safe for users of all ages because no data is collected or transmitted.

Your Rights and Choices

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual squads or sessions within the app
  • Uninstalling the app (removes all data permanently)

Data Export

Currently, SquadShuffle does not offer a data export feature. All data remains on your device and is not accessible outside the app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any material changes, we will notify you by:

  • Updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy
  • Posting a notice in the app (if applicable)
  • Updating the policy on our website

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

International Users

SquadShuffle is available worldwide. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no cross-border data transfer concerns. All data stays on your device regardless of your location.
